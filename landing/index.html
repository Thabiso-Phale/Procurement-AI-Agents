Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything founders ask before they start.

Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
Everything — your suppliers, purchase orders, invoices, contracts — is stored in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers. We literally cannot see your data.
What happens if I clear my browser or switch devices?
Your data lives in the browser you used to set it up. If you clear site data, it will be erased. We strongly recommend using Settings → Export Backup weekly and saving the file to Google Drive or OneDrive. Restoring takes seconds.
Do I need to install anything?
No. ProjectBuys runs entirely in your browser — no download, no IT setup, no admin rights required. Open app.projectbuys.com and you're ready to go.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No credit card, no sign-up, no email required. The Free plan is genuinely free — just open the app and start using it. You only need to pay if you choose to upgrade to Grow or Team.
Is there a limit on purchase orders and invoices?
No. Purchase orders and invoices are unlimited on every plan — Free, Grow, and Team. The only limits on the Free plan are the number of suppliers (up to 3) and AI requests (30 per day).
Can my team use it together?
The Free and Grow plans are single-user. The Team plan ($89/mo) supports up to 10 seats with shared data, role-based access, and approval workflows — so your whole ops team can work from the same system.
Can I put my company logo and address on documents?
Yes. Go to Settings → Company Profile & Branding to add your address, phone, email, website, and VAT number — these print on every PO and RFQ at no cost. Logo upload is available on the Grow and Team plans.
Does it work on mobile?
The app is responsive and works on tablets and mobile browsers. For heavy data entry (adding suppliers, building POs) a laptop or desktop is more comfortable, but reviewing dashboards and approving requests works well on mobile.
What AI model does it use?
ProjectBuys is powered by Claude by Anthropic — one of the most capable and safety-focused AI models available. You can use our hosted key (30 requests/day free) or add your own Anthropic API key in Settings for unlimited use on any plan.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your billing portal at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free. Your data stays in your browser regardless.
