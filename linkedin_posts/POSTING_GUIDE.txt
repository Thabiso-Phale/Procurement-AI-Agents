HOW TO SCHEDULE A PERSONAL FEED POST (LinkedIn native):
  1. Go to linkedin.com/feed
  2. Click "Start a post"

  4. Paste the post copy below
  5. Click the CLOCK icon (bottom left of composer)
  6. Pick the date and time → click "Schedule"

  3. Upload the image → paste the copy → Post
